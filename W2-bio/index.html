My Biography

Prior Cyber Network & Security education I took classes full time at ECPI University in Virginia Beach, Virginia. I am currently enrolled in Cyber Security Progam at New England Institute of Technology and after completing my degree program I plan to complete a higher degree in Criminal Justice.
