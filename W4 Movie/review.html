REVIEW

Avengers: Age of Ultron

Stephen Colprit

August 7th, 2023

"Stark and Banner discover an artificial intelligence within the scepter's gem, and secretly decide to use it to complete Stark's "Ultron" global defense program. The unexpectedly sentient Ultron, believing he must eradicate humanity to save Earth, eliminates Stark's A.I. J.A.R.V.I.S. and attacks the Avengers at their headquarters. Escaping with the scepter, Ultron uses the resources in Strucker's Sokovia base to upgrade his rudimentary body and build an army of robot drones." - WordPress

In the movie Avengers: Age of Ultron there is a race against time to save earth from an Enemy called Ultron tries to extinct humans from earth from Technology that went bad where Artifical Intelligence turned against its creators. Tony Stark (Iron Man) awakened this enemy when he restarted up a peacekeeping program and was experimenting without his comrades knowlege and this dangerous new enemy Ultron is created from Artifical Intelligence And wants to impose absolute power on earth. After this threat emerges Tony Stark assembles the Avengers to try to destroy these highly Technological Beasts and save the planet. In the end Earth was saved from a metorite and Ultron's Plan is defeated, The Avengers are disassembled Tony Stark takes off to think about what he has done, Hulk flies into space to get over his ex,Thor takes back off to Asgard to do investigative work on phase 3: infinity stones, Captain America and Black Widow are left to pick up the pieces, Hawkeye goes to his family because he made a promise to his wife after he saved the world he would return home.

"I like this movie because it shows fantasy reality of a type of futuristic superhero team battling together to defeat enemies. I like the action packed design of the story line. I think the drama, humour and acting is great by all characters. I like the hight-tech outline of the narrative that the movie is based and the history of the Marvel Comics and the new redesigned narratives. The graphics make this movie stand out and make the scenes come to life. The Avengers are a super hero group designed to help people and stop enemeys from destroying the planet. This movie does not follow the 2011 - 2015 Marvel Comic books storyline issues #1 throught #10/#10A.I. Although having following the previous comic book titles and characters, the inspiration is different and not adapted from the books, the directos wanted something different. The aspect of having the all the commic book superheors together in the film makes for an action packed movie."
