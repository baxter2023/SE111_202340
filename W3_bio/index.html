My Biography

Hello, my name is Stephen Colprit. I am an Honorably Discharged Disabled Veteran. I was an Active Duty Seabee in the United States Navy where I was injured during a humanitarian mission in South America in 2008 where I was crushed by a piece of heavy equipment machinery. I have had numerous deployments in the Middle East Centcom Area of operations including tours in Bahrain and Jordan and as a private contrator in Afghanistan, Kuwait, Dubai & Liberia, Africa. I recently graduated Bay State College in Boston with an Associate of Science Degree in Criminal Justice. Prior education in Cyber Network & Security where I took classes full time at ECPI University in Virginia Beach, Virginia. I am currently enrolled in Cyber Security Bachelor Progam at New England Institute of Technology and after completing my degree program I plan to complete a higher education degree in Criminal Justice. Upon finishing my education I would like to become a Defense contractor and work with the military.
